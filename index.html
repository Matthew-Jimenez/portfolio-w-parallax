About

Frontend deverloper with 3 years of programming experience and 1+ years of developing full featured frontend web applications. Extremely motivated to learn as much as possible about the full spectrum of software development. Proficient with React, Redux, modern JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Seeking to join a company as a Frontend Developer with the ability to grow into the backend end as well.

Its hard to explain the passion I have towards programming. Theres just something about going from the beginning to the end of a project that exites me. Perhaps the most satisfying aspect of the process is that no matter how well you performed the previous time, theres always room to improve, alternate ways of going about solving issues, and a vast ocean of untreaded waters to dive into.

I take pride in my work period. I love producing highly organized readble code and I am always willing to take notes on becoming better at this. I am currently looking for new opportunities in the Los Angeles County area and hope to find a team that could use a Frontend Developer with the ability to grow into the backend as well.
